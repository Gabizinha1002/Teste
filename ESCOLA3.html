About

Welcome to Estaport Secondary School. We are a secondary school and sixth form College located in Lagos, Nigeria. We educate based on the Nigerian and West African curricula, and we are in partnership with NCC Education UK. This partnership enables us provide British Education resulting in British Diplomas and Certificates that are globally acceptable.The world has changed, and so have we. We offer our students a first-class educational experience using technology as an important tool in teaching. We have partnered with international educators to provide a deeper technological education than what is conventionally provided. Our teachers are sound experts and communicators, preparing students for success in internal & external examinations. We instill the values of integrity, diligence, and courage in students and staff alike. Our spacious grounds, and passionate instructors provide a ripe environment for sporting talents to emerge.

The Proprietor



It is my privilege to welcome you to our school. We have upgraded our curriculum and teaching methods and are adopting cutting edge technology to our unique program and introduce the digital education of ESTAPORT. Estaport Secondary School is the result of several journeys of endeavour, including but not limited to parents, staff, students and stakeholders , which led us down this path. Our desire for the 21st Century education development unfolds as we make our teachers and students rise to their next level of learning! This is why we are at the forefront of digital education. This is the new normal, and we roll with the new normal. Students are discovering new ways to interact with their interests online and through the digital media. They are no longer confined to just physical texts when reading or learning more about their possible interests. With digital literacy, a child’s ability to succeed both in school and throughout his/her life is defined. In view of these, Estaport Secondary School is an accredited partner of the NCC Education UK, that would lead to an award of the British qualifications. We offer the full Nigerian curricular, validated for WAEC, NECO, BECE and JAMB, on the state and federal levels. Estaport Secondary School dares to be different by offering a Virtual Learning Environment(VLE) and a team of dedicated professionals working together for the benefit of our children and families. These are available to as many people who would want to make use of these opportunities. I encourage you to take some time – explore our website and read more about our history, our philosophy, our programmes, and our community. Once more, welcome to Estaport Secondary School.




Partnership With NCC Education Estaport Secondary School was recently accredited as a partner learning centre by NCC Education. Originally established as a computing initiative by the British Government in 1966, NCC Education is a UK awarding body and global provider of education. Since inception, NCC has developed a network of over 200 partner centres located in more than 50 countries.
