Learning at home

Welcome to the website to help you to help your child during school closure.

In response to your answers to the recent survey, this website focusses on those areas that most concern you and your child.

No-one expects you to replace your child’s teacher; our schools will manage their learning. Just seek a balance between learning at home, working from home, running your home and having fun!
